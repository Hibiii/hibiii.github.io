A Quilt mod that adds plushies for cuddling.

This mod was designed because yes. I wanted to be cute in the game and I thought I should get an IKEA shark in game and hold it tight because they're soft and cute!

When you craft or rename a plushie, it'll have your username attached to it.

This mod is required on both the server and the client.

The Official Quilt Mod.
