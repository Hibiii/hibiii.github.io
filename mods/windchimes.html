--- title: Windchimes description: Gentle chimes that sway in the wind. icon: /assets/icon/windchimes.svg ---

This is a lightweight mod that adds wind chimes to the game! They sway in the wind, and aren't just static blocks, they are chimes and they do chime - and even more if it's raining.

So far, there's only iron pipe chimes, copper pipe chimes, and bamboo chimes. More are to come, maybe also perhaps a dreamcatcher? Here are the crafting recipses:

Additional Information

Regarding Quilt: Wind​chimes will support Quilt once I'm comfortable with porting mods to it. Fabric support will secondary, and may be dropped.

Requirements

  • Fabric Loader or compatible
  • Fabric API on CurseForge and on Modrinth or compatible
